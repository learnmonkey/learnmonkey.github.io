About

What is Learnmonkey?

Learnmonkey is a free and open-source website dedicated to making free and high-quality educational content. Learnmonkey is licensed under the BSD 3-Clause License and is made using Jekyll.

Our Mission

At Learnmonkey, we strive to provide high-quality educational content for free.

History

Learnmonkey was created in January 2022.
