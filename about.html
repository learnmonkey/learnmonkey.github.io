History

Contributing

To contribute to Learnmonkey, simply go to the GitHub repository of Learnmonkey. There, you can make issues and pull requests.

Donating

Learnmonkey doesn't have ads and will remain without ads. Currently, we do not accept donations, but in the future we will. In the meantime, consider contributing to a local charity.
