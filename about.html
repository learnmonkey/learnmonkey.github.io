About

What is Learnmonkey?

Learnmonkey is a free and open-source website dedicated to making free and high-quality educational content.

Our Mission

At Learnmonkey, we strive to provide high-quality educational content for free.

History

Learnmonkey was created in January 2022.

Contributing

To contribute to Learnmonkey, simply go to the GitHub repository of Learnmonkey. There, you can make issues and pull requests.

Translating

Currently, Learnmonkey is only available in English, but we are currently working on a translation in Chinese.

Donating

Learnmonkey doesn't have ads and will remain without ads. Currently, we do not accept donations, but in the future we will. In the meantime, consider contributing to a local charity and sharing Learnmonkey with your friends!

Learnmonkey is Open Source!

Learnmonkey is an open source project and it is licensed under the BSD 3-Clause License.
