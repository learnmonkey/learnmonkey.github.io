Introduction to Ruby

What is Ruby?

Ruby is a dynamic programming language with a simple and easy-to-read style syntax. It's built open-source with a focus and simplicity and productivity.

Why Learn Ruby?

Ruby is easy to pick up, with simple syntax. In fact, someone with no programming experience could even understand some of Ruby's syntax. It's great alternative to Python, and allows you to work with many framewirjs, the most popular one being Ruby on Rails.
