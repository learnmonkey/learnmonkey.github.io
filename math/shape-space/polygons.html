Polygons

In this tutorial, we'll learn about Polygons.

What are Polygons?

A polygon is a closed 2-D shape that has straight sides. There are two kinds of Polygons: Regular Polygons and Irregular Polygons.

Regular Polygons

Regular Polygons are Polygons in which all sides lengths and interior angles are the same.

Irregular Polygons

Irregular Polygons are Polygons in which all sides lengths and interior angles are not the same.
