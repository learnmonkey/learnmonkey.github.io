Introduction to Shape and Space

In this tutorial, we'll learn about Shape and Space, which is very similar to Geometry.

What is Shape and Space?

In math, Shape and Space deals with shapes and the Cartesian Plane. We apply transformations on different shapes and it includes all different aspects of Geometry, which is basically the same as Shape and Space.

Why Learn Shape and Space?

Shape and Space is very important to learn. It's required in school math curriculums and comes in handy in life, like calculating the dimensions of wood.
