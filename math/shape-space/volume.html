Volume

In this tutorial, we'll learn about Volume of 3-D shapes.

What is Volume?

Volume is similar to area but it's for 3-D shapes. It is the amount of space a 3-D shape takes up.

Calculating Volume of Recangular Prisms

To calculate volume for rectangular prisms, you just need to multiply width, height, and length, which are the three main values of a rectangular prism.
