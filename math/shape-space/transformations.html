Transformations

What are Transformations?

Transformation is very important in Shape and Space. It involves moving different shapes, both 2D and 3D, in different ways. In this tutorial, we'll learn about them.

Different Transformations

There are three main transformations:

  • The Translation
  • The Reflection
  • The Rotation

Of course, there are also some other Transformations like Dilations but these are the main ones.
