Resistance

Resistance is a measure of how much something blocks electricity. You can think of resistance as friction for electricity.

Since we don't live in a perfect world, everything has resistance. Even the wires have resistance, albeit very low resistance.

We can add resistance on purpose with resistors. Resistors are can be used for limiting the amount of current when dealing with sensitive electrical components, such as LEDs.

Fun fact: resistors have colored bars on them which depend on the resistance.
