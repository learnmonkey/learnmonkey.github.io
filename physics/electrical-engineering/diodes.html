Diodes

What is a Diode?

Diodes are electrical components that block the flow of electricity in one direction, but allow it in the opposite direction. The direction in which the diode blocks electricity is called the reverse direction, while the opposite direction is called the forward direction.

Uses

One interesting use of diodes is as a light. LEDs are in fact a specialized type of diode.
