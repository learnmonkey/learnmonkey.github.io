Introduction to Electrical Engineering

What is Electrical Engineering?

Electrical engineering deals with electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. As you'd imagine, electrical engineering is incredibly important to all aspects of our lives.

Why Learn Electrical Engineering?

Electricity and electronics are fundamental to our lives and it's good to know how they work. Also, electrical engineering is in many curriculums worldwide.
